Chen Li

Chen Li (born 26 July 1990) is a Chinese folk song singer, independent musician, singer-songwriter, and former lead singer of the Dreamer Band (空想家乐队). She was born in Guiyang, Guizhou and graduated from Shanghai University of International Business and Economics.
